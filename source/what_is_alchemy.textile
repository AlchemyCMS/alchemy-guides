h2. What is Alchemy CMS?

Alchemy is not a common CMS. Alchemy is rather a CMS framework. It enables you to build your own CMS.

The name is program: Alchemy CMS is a kind of chemical construction kit for developers that want to build professional web-applications in a very short time.

Also content managers are enthusiastic about Alchemy CMS because of its clear and powerful user interface that is very easy to use and intuitive to learn.
